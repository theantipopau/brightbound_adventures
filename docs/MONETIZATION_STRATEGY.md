# BrightBound Adventures: Ethical Monetization Strategy

**Goal**: Sustainable revenue without compromising educational value or user experience

---

## Monetization Philosophy

✅ **DO**:
- Add genuine value
- Respect user privacy
- Maintain educational integrity
- Provide free core experience
- Be transparent about pricing
- Prioritize learning outcomes

❌ **DON'T**:
- Exploit kids or parents
- Use manipulative tactics
- Interrupt learning flow
- Collect excessive data
- Use predatory pricing
- Compromise content quality

---

- Progress tracking

## What NOT to Do

❌ **Avoid**:
1. **Aggressive ads** - Especially problematic for kids
2. **Pay-to-progress** - Gating learning by cost (unethical)
3. **Loot boxes/random rewards** - Exploitative mechanics
4. **Dark patterns** - Confusing pricing, hidden costs
5. **Data harvesting** - Excessive tracking for monetization
6. **Energy systems** - "Wait 30 mins to play again"
7. **Cosmetic-only premium** - Users feel bled dry
8. **Constant notifications** - Manipulative engagement tactics

## Key Metrics to Track

**If Monetization is Working**:
- DAU (Daily Active Users): Growing
- Retention Day 7: 30%+
- Retention Day 30: 15%+
- Premium conversion: 8-15%
- Customer LTV > 3x CAC (customer acquisition cost)

- Parent satisfaction: 4.5+ stars

**Red Flags**:
- Rising churn after paywall introduction
- Negative app store reviews about pricing
- Declining engagement in free tier
- Users gaming the system to avoid premium
