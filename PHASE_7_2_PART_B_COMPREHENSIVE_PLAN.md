## System-Wide Patterns Observed

### Strengths (from initial audit sample)
1. ✅ **Clear question structure** - Well-formed, unambiguous questions
2. ✅ **Good explanations** - Comprehensive feedback for wrong answers
3. ✅ **Helpful hints** - Guiding students to correct answers
4. ✅ **Progression** - Difficulty distributions reasonable
5. ✅ **Coverage** - Broad representation of skills

### Weaknesses (from initial audit sample)
1. ⚠️ **Context gap** (24% flagged) - Questions often academic, not scenario-based
2. ⚠️ **Cognitive imbalance** - Heavy on Remember/Understand, light on Create
3. ⚠️ **Content gaps** - Some topics under-represented
4. ⚠️ **Real-world integration** - Limited authentic learning scenarios
5. ⚠️ **Standards alignment** - ACARA codes missing from many questions

### Content Gaps Identified

**Critical (High Priority)**:
- Decimals operations (only 15 questions for Year 4-6)
- Data interpretation (only 10+ questions, under-represented)
- Geometry advanced (limited 3D and transformation content)
- Higher-order thinking (Evaluate/Create levels across all modules)

**Important (Medium Priority)**:
- Complex measurement scenarios
- Advanced fraction operations
- Comprehension passage variety
- Problem-solving multi-step questions

**Desirable (Low Priority)**:
- Context variety (multiple real-world scenarios per skill)
- Cultural representation in questions
- Connection to student interests
