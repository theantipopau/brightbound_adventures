The Team

BrightBound Adventures is developed by Matt Hurley, a passionate developer dedicated to creating meaningful educational experiences for children. The project combines expertise in game design, educational psychology, and child development to create something truly special.

Curriculum Alignment

Our content is carefully crafted to align with the Australian Curriculum (ACARA v9.0) and specifically addresses areas identified in NAPLAN assessments. This means children are building skills that directly support their school learning while having fun.

Key areas covered include:

  • Literacy: Phonics, reading comprehension, grammar, and vocabulary
  • Numeracy: Number sense, operations, fractions, and word problems
  • Critical Thinking: Logic, patterns, and problem-solving
  • Communication: Storytelling and creative expression
  • Motor Skills: Hand-eye coordination and reaction time
