Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Overview

BrightBound Adventures ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our educational game application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

The short version: We don't collect any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Don't Collect

BrightBound Adventures is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Photos, videos, or voice recordings
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Any data about your child

Local Data Storage

BrightBound Adventures stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Player Profiles: Character names and avatar customisations
  • Game Progress: Completed activities, scores, and achievements
  • Settings: Sound preferences and other app settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers. If you uninstall the app, this data may be permanently deleted.

No Internet Required

BrightBound Adventures works completely offline. The app does not require an internet connection to function, and no data is ever sent to or received from external servers during gameplay.

No Advertising

BrightBound Adventures contains no advertisements of any kind. We do not use advertising networks, tracking pixels, or any third-party advertising services.

No Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services that could collect data about users. This includes:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

BrightBound Adventures is designed for children ages 4-12 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Since we don't collect any personal information from anyone, including children, there is no personal data to protect, share, or delete.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, the security of your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend using device passcodes and keeping your device's operating system up to date.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: hello@matthurley.dev

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. However, you can delete all locally stored game data by uninstalling the app from your device.
