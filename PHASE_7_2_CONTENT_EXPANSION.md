# Phase 7.2: Content Expansion & Adaptive Logic

## 1. Adaptive Difficulty System
The **Number Nebula** (Numeracy) generator has been upgraded with an adaptive logic engine.

### How it works:
Instead of generating questions of a single difficulty, the system now creates a pedagogical progression.
- **Level 1**: 100% Easy (Focus on building confidence).
- **Level 2**: 70% Easy, 30% Medium (Introduction to harder concepts).
- **Level 3**: 10% Warmup, 70% Core Medium, 20% Challenge (Balanced).
- **Level 5**: 100% Hard/Expert (Mastery).

This ensures that players are constantly challenged but not overwhelmed.

## 2. New Question Varieties Added
We have significantly expanded the "Question Bank" with new types of procedural questions:

### A. Time & Clocks (Easy/Medium)
- **Concept**: Reading analog clock descriptions.
- **Example**: "What time is it? 🕒 3:30" -> Answer: "Half past 3".
- **Logic**: Generates random hours and half-hours with text descriptions.

### B. Geometry & Shapes (Hard)
- **Concept**: Knowledge of shape properties (sides).
- **Example**: "How many sides does a Pentagon have?" -> Answer: "5".
- **Shapes**: Triangles, Squares, Rectangles, Pentagons, Hexagons, Octagons.

### C. Algebraic Thinking (Medium)
- **Concept**: Finding the missing part in an equation.
- **Example**: "? + 5 = 12. What number goes in the ?" -> Answer: "7".
- **Logic**: Randomly generates target sums and missing addends.

## 3. Existing Varieties Enhanced
- **Addition/Subtraction**: Now fully localized with Australian concepts (Lamingtons, Kangaroos) via the NAPLAN integration.
- **Word Problems**: Better variety in number ranges.
